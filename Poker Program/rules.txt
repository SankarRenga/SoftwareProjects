Glossary of terms:
Check: Player continues his/her turn with no bet or raise
Flop: The first three cards dealt
Showdown: When players reveal their hands to determine the winner
Fold: Pass for the round
Check: No bet on your turn continue to next player
Raise: Add more money onto the present bet
Call: Agree to a raise or bet placed

Poker Hands:
Royal Flush:	 	
A straight from a ten to an ace with all five cards of the same suit. In Debonair poker all suits are ranked equally.
Straight Flush:	 	
Any straight with all five cards of the same suit.
Four of a Kind:	 	
Any four cards of the same rank. If two players share the same Four of a Kind (on the board), 
the bigger fifth card determines the winner
Full House:	 	
Any three cards of the same rank together with any two cards of the same rank. 
Flush:	 	
Any five cards of the same suit (not consecutive). The highest card of the five determines the rank of the flush.
Straight:	 	
Any five consecutive cards of different suits. Aces can count as either a high or a low card.
Three of a Kind	 	
Any three cards of the same rank. 
Two Pair:	 	
Any two cards of the same rank together with another two cards of the same rank. 
One Pair:	 	
Any two cards of the same rank. 
High Card:	 	
Any hand that is not mentioned above.
